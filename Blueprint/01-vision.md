# STRATA

## Tagline

Where Ideas Become Engineering.

---

## Vision

STRATA is an AI-native engineering operating system that transforms ideas into production-ready software through structured research, architecture, planning, implementation, verification, and persistent project intelligence.

Unlike traditional AI assistants that answer isolated questions, STRATA understands an entire project as a continuously evolving system. It maintains context, remembers engineering decisions, tracks progress, and guides developers throughout the complete software development lifecycle.

The goal is not to replace engineers.

The goal is to reduce engineering friction while preserving engineering judgment.

---

## Mission

Reduce engineering friction without reducing engineering judgment.

---

## Product Philosophy

### Research Before Implementation

Every successful project begins by understanding the problem before writing code.

Research should become part of the engineering workflow.

---

### Design Before Code

Architecture should always precede implementation.

Implementation is the consequence of good design.

---

### Persistent Project Intelligence

Projects should accumulate knowledge instead of repeatedly rebuilding context.

---

### Engineering Over Prompting

Developers should focus on engineering decisions.

AI implementation agents execute engineering specifications.

---

### Human-Centered Automation

Automation should remove repetitive work while preserving human ownership of important technical decisions.

---

## Product Principles

- **Persistent by Default**: System state, decision history, and project memory are preserved across sessions to maintain context continuously.
- **Modular by Design**: Elements of the system are loosely coupled, enabling clear boundaries and replaceable components.
- **Extensible First**: The core engine supports custom plugins, adapters, and tools to integrate seamlessly with diverse developer workflows.
- **AI as an Engineering Partner**: Automation runs inside defined engineering guardrails, maintaining human oversight of critical decisions.
- **Quality is Built-In**: Verification processes are baked into the execution lifecycle, preventing regression and ensuring high-fidelity outputs.

---

## Primary Audience

Version 1 focuses on:

- AI Hackathon Teams
- Engineering Students
- Independent Builders

Future versions may expand toward startups and engineering teams.

---

## Non-Goals

STRATA is NOT:

- A chatbot
- An IDE
- A code editor
- A no-code platform
- A replacement for software engineers

---

## Long-Term Vision

STRATA should evolve into an engineering operating system.

Interfaces such as MCP, IDE agents, CLI, and REST APIs are adapters built on top of the same engineering engine.

---

## Success Definition

Developers stop asking:

"What should I ask the AI?"

Instead they ask:

"Continue my project."
